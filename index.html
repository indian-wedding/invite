The Venues

Two magnificent settings, one unforgettable celebration across Gajraula, Uttar Pradesh.

🏛️

Highway Resort

Grand Wedding Venue · Gajraula

The crown jewel of the celebrations. With sweeping banquet lawns, regal interiors and impeccable hospitality, Highway Resort sets a majestic stage for the most sacred moment of our lives — the Wedding ceremony under a starlit sky.

💍 Wedding · 19 June, Evening
📍 NH-9, Highway Resort, Gajraula, Amroha District, Uttar Pradesh — ~110 km from Delhi via NH-9
🌿

Moga Punjabi Tadka

Pre-Wedding Celebrations · Gajraula

Where the festivities begin! Moga Punjabi hosts our vibrant pre-wedding ceremonies — from the colour-soaked Mehendi evening to the electrifying Sangeet night filled with dance, music, and memories for a lifetime.

🌿 Mehendi & 🎵 Sangeet · 18 June, Evening 🌼 Haldi · 19 June, Morning
📍 Moga Punjabi Tadka, Gajraula, Amroha District, Uttar Pradesh — nearby Highway Resort
Getting There & Staying
  • 🚗By Road: Connected via NH-9 (Delhi–Moradabad). ~110 km from Delhi (~2.5 hrs). Ample parking at both venues.
  • 🚂By Train: Gajraula Junction is the nearest railway station. Regular trains from Delhi (Anand Vihar) & Lucknow.
  • ✈️By Air: Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi (~130 km). Book flights early and arrange a cab from Delhi.
  • 🏨Accommodation: Resort rooms available at Highway Resort. Additional hotels in Gajraula & Moradabad (20 km). Contact us for coordination.
