The Venue

✦

A beautiful setting for a beautiful day.

Highway Resort

📍 Gajraula, Uttar Pradesh

Nestled in the serene landscape of Gajraula, Highway Resort offers a breathtaking backdrop for our wedding celebrations. With lush gardens, grand halls, and impeccable hospitality, this venue sets the perfect stage for the most important day of our lives. The sprawling grounds will host our wedding under the stars — a truly magical evening awaits.

Getting There & Staying

🚗 By Road: Gajraula is well connected via NH-9 (Delhi–Moradabad highway). Approximately 110 km from Delhi (~2.5 hrs).
🚂 By Train: Nearest railway station is Gajraula Junction. Regular trains from Delhi (Anand Vihar) and Lucknow.
✈️ By Air: Nearest airport is Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi (~130 km). Book early!
🏨 Accommodation: The resort itself has rooms available. Please contact us for group booking details and special rates for guests.
